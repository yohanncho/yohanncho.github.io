The Pump Action


The Pump Action has been the flagship product in a venture I co-founded called DE Innovations Inc. which specializes in full circle e-commerce and product design. This process included customer interviews to identify needs, generate various puncturing concepts and design for manufacturing. We launched a Kickstarter and raised $12,600. used CAD software to model and revise over 50 iterations and have sold over 3000 products to date.


Some key experiences include:

  • Led talks with two overseas injection molding manufacturers to negotiate prices and payment terms, develop assembly procedures, quality control checks and scale production
  • Filed a US and Canadian patent; developed a bilingual manufacturing contract and an NNN to ensure supplier accountability
  • Incorporated the business, outsourced fulfillment to the USA and handled partnerships with companies in the alcohol industry
